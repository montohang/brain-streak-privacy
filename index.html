Privacy Policy - Brain Streak

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview

Brain Streak ("we", "our", or "the app") is a memory pattern game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

Brain Streak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data (scores, statistics, badges, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — to collect anonymous crash reports for improving app stability. No personal information is collected.
  • Firebase Analytics — to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., game mode played, levels reached). No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

Brain Streak displays ads through Google AdMob. You may see banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. Ad networks may use cookies or device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Brain Streak is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
